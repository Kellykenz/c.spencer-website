1:1 PRIVATE COACHING

Tailored one-to-one sessions designed to match your individual goals - whether you're learning from scratch or refining competitive skills. Sessions focus on technique, footwork, and strategy to build confidence and mastery.

GROUP LESSONS

A fun, interactive way to train with peers while improving consistency, coordination, and fitness. Encourages teamwork and social connection while keeping training cost-friendly.

High performance Coaching

For serious players preparing for tournaments. Focuses on tactical play, match strategy, physical conditioning, and mental resilience.

Corporate Tennis & FITNESS PACKAGES

Special sessions for professionals who want to stay fit, network, and de-stress through tennis. Ideal for companies that promote wellness and team building.

Kid's Summer Tennis Camp

A seasonal program that blends fun and learning for young players. Kids are grouped by age and skill level:

  • 3-7 years: Red Ball
  • 7-9 years: Orange Ball
  • 10-12 years: Green Ball

    • The camp focuses on fundamentals, coordination, sportmanship, and character building.
