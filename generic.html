Who Am I?

Breeding future champions - coach spencer

COMFORT SPENCER

I'm Coach Comfort Spencer, founder of C.Spencer Tennis Pro Concepts - a passionate tennis coach, national player, and certified PTR & ITF professional dedicated to shaping the next generation of tennis champions.

My tennis journey began long before I became a coach - as young girl with big dreams, a racket, and relentless determination. From those early days on court to representing Nigeria at international tournaments, tennis has always been more than a sport to me; it's been a calling. Over the years, I've had the privilege of transforming that passion into purpose - coaching kids, adults, and aspiring professionals to develop not just skills, but the mindset of winners.

My coaching philosophy

At C.Spencer Tennis Pro Concepts, I believe every player - regardless of age or experience - carries a spark of greatness. My approach is built on *Personalized training tailored to each player's strengths and pace. *Fun engaging lessons that make learning exciting for kids and adults alike. *Strong focus on fundamentals, discipline, and mindset - the foundation of champions

Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or training to compete, my goal is to help you unlock your potential and fall in love with the process of growth.
